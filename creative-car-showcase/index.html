// DESIGN STAGES

THE CREATIVE JOURNEY

How a sketch becomes an ultra-high performance production reality.

STAGE 01

CONCEPT DESIGN & FLUID SKETCHES

Our design process begins with digital sculpts and virtual clay modelling, shaping natural fluid air tunnels through the chassis for maximal ground effect downforce.

STAGE 02

WIND TUNNEL TESTING & DIGITAL CFD

Supercomputing clusters run thousands of Computational Fluid Dynamics tests, leading to functional carbon ducts, active wings, and a drag coefficient under 0.20 Cd.

STAGE 03

SOLID-STATE BATTERY PACK DEVELOPMENT

Moving beyond lithium-ion, we engineered solid-state battery modules integrated directly into the monocoque structure, providing 300% more energy density and 10-minute quick charge.

STAGE 04

THE APEX ONE PRODUCTION

Combining additive carbon manufacturing with precision electric motor integration, the Apex One rolls out to global reservation holders, setting new benchmark lap records.
